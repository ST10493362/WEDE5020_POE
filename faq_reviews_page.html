Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are your wigs made from real human hair?

A: We offer both 100% human hair wigs and high-quality synthetic options.

Q: How do I choose the right wig cap size?

A: Use a soft measuring tape to measure the circumference of your head. We provide a sizing chart on every product page.

Q: Can I heat style your wigs?

A: Yes! Our human hair wigs can be styled with heat tools. For synthetic wigs, check the product details—some are heat-resistant.

Q: How long will my wig last?

A: With proper care, human hair wigs can last over a year. Synthetic wigs typically last 4–6 months.

Q: What’s your return policy?

A: We accept returns within 14 days for unworn, unstyled wigs with tags attached.
